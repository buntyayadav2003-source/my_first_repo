Krishna Janmashtami

Friday, September 4, 2026

Krishna Janmashtami, also known as Gokulashtami, is a major Hindu festival celebrating the birth of Lord Krishna, the eighth avatar of Lord Vishnu. It is observed on the eighth day of the dark fortnight in the Hindu month of Bhadrapada (August–September). The festival marks the triumph of good over evil and celebrates divine love.
